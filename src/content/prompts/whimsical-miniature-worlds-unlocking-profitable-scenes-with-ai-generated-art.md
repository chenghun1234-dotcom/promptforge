To effectively monetize images generated with this prompt, consider specializing in creating a series of these miniature worlds, each with its unique theme, such as seasonal villages or fantastical landscapes. Then, leverage platforms like Adobe Stock or Shutterstock for stock photo sales, emphasizing the exclusivity and detail of each piece.
