## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a popular trend in the world of digital fashion, and Zepeto is one of the leading platforms for creators to showcase and sell their unique designs. With the help of AI generation, you can create stunning, futuristic outfits that appeal to a wide range of customers.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you need to craft a well-structured prompt that includes specific details about the desired design. The main prompt should include elements like:
* Futuristic inspiration
* Neon-lit accents
* Metallic textures
* Sleek, high-tech accessories

The negative prompt should exclude elements that don't fit the cyberpunk aesthetic, such as:
* Overly simplistic designs
* Bright colors
* Lack of futuristic elements

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the best results, use the following technical settings:
* **Sampler:** Euler a
* **CFG:** 7.5
* **Steps:** 50

These settings will allow you to generate detailed, high-quality images that showcase your cyberpunk cyberwear designs.
