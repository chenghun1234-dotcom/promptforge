## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
The key to generating stunning cyberpunk cyberwear with AI lies in the prompt. A well-crafted prompt should inspire the AI to produce outfits that are not only visually striking but also deeply rooted in cyberpunk aesthetics. 

- **Main Prompt:** `Generate a high-fashion cyberpunk outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon accents, metallic textures, and futuristic accessories.`
- **Negative Prompt:** `Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of detail, or elements that are not futuristic or cyberpunk-inspired.`

The main prompt encourages the AI to incorporate essential cyberpunk elements such as neon accents and metallic textures, while the negative prompt ensures that the generated outfits are complex and aligned with the cyberpunk theme.
