## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a highly sought-after theme in the world of Zepeto, offering creators an opportunity to showcase their skills in designing futuristic, high-tech outfits. To succeed in this niche, it's essential to understand the key elements of cyberpunk fashion and how to effectively utilize AI prompts to create stunning digital assets.

## Crafting the Perfect AI Prompt
The AI prompt is the foundation of your cyberwear design. It should be detailed, yet flexible enough to allow the AI model to generate unique and interesting results. The main prompt should include the following elements:
* Theme: Cyberpunk
* Character: Zepeto
* Outfit: Specify the type of outfit, such as a jacket, pants, and boots
* Colors: Define the primary and accent colors, such as black, neon green, and metallic
* Textures: Describe the desired textures, such as sleek, metallic, and glowing

The negative prompt is equally important, as it helps to refine the results by excluding unwanted elements. In this case, the negative prompt should avoid:
* Bright colors
* Cartoonish designs
* Overly simplistic textures
* Logos or branding

## Technical Settings for Optimal Results
To achieve the best possible results, it's crucial to adjust the technical settings according to your prompt. The recommended settings for this prompt are:
* Sampler: Euler a

These settings will allow the AI model to generate highly detailed and realistic results, while also providing a good balance between creativity and control.
