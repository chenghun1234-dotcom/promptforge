## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a popular trend in the world of Zepeto, with many users looking for unique and stylish outfits to enhance their characters. As a professional creator, you can capitalize on this trend by creating stunning cyberpunk-inspired designs using AI.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you need to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI model. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of the design, such as:
* Futuristic and cyberpunk-inspired aesthetic
* Neon-lit accents and metallic textures
* Sleek lines and wearable technology elements
* Augmented reality elements

The negative prompt should avoid elements that are not consistent with the cyberpunk aesthetic, such as:
* Overly complex designs or too much clutter
* Bright colors or childish patterns
* Elements that are not futuristic or technological
