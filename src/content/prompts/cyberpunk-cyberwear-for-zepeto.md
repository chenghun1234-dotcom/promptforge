## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you need to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI model. The main prompt should include key elements such as:
* A specific theme or style (cyberpunk)
* A description of the desired outfit (sleek black jacket, metallic pants, high-tech boots)
* A focus on details and textures (intricate designs, neon accents)

The negative prompt should exclude unwanted elements, such as:
* Bright colors or cartoonish designs
* Simplistic textures or lack of details
* Logos or branding
