To create a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto character, use the following prompt:
"Create a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto character, including a sleek black jacket with neon green accents, a pair of high-tech pants with built-in circuitry, and a pair of futuristic boots with glowing blue soles."
