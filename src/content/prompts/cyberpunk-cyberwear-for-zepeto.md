As a professional creator of digital assets, tapping into the vibrant world of cyberpunk fashion can be a lucrative venture, especially on platforms like Zepeto where users crave unique and stylish outfits for their characters. ### Crafting the Perfect Prompt

To create compelling cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be precise and evocative. Consider the following elements:

- **Highly Detailed and Futuristic:** Emphasize the need for intricate, high-tech details that scream cyberpunk, such as neon lights, holographic displays, or metallic plating.
- **Neon Accents and Metallic Textures:** These are hallmark elements of cyberpunk design, adding a layer of depth and futurism to your outfits.
- **Avant-Garde Accessories:** Think beyond traditional clothing items and include accessories like futuristic headpieces, cybernetic limbs, or high-tech backpacks.
- **Sleek, High-Tech Design Elements:** Incorporate elements that reflect a futuristic, cutting-edge aesthetic, such as streamlined silhouettes, reflective surfaces, or glow-in-the-dark accents.

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results

Achieving the desired level of detail and style in your cyberpunk cyberwear requires careful adjustment of your AI generation settings:

- **Sampler:** Euler a offers a good balance between detail and diversity, making it a solid choice for intricate cyberpunk designs.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A setting of 7.5 provides a strong guidance towards the prompt without over-constraining the generation, allowing for creative freedom within the cyberpunk theme.
- **Steps:** 50 steps can provide a good balance between generation speed and detail, though you may need to adjust based on the complexity of your prompt and the desired outcome.
