For the best results, use the following technical settings:

- **Steps:** 50 to ensure a thorough and detailed generation process that captures the complexity of cyberpunk designs.

### Creating Your Asset
1. **Prompt:** Use the provided main prompt to generate your cyberpunk cyberwear: "Generate a highly detailed, futuristic cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto avatar, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and intricate circuitry patterns, with a focus on avant-garde fashion and sleek, high-tech aesthetics."
