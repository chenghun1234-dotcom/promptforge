## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
As a professional creator of digital assets, staying ahead of the curve in terms of style and demand is crucial. The cyberpunk genre, with its blend of futuristic technology and gritty, high-tech aesthetics, offers a rich tapestry for designing captivating cyberwear for platforms like Zepeto. This guide will walk you through the process of creating stunning cyberpunk cyberwear using AI, from crafting the perfect prompt to monetizing your digital assets.

## Crafting Your AI Prompt
The key to generating high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear lies in your AI prompt. You need a prompt that is both descriptive and focused, guiding the AI to produce an outfit that embodies the essence of cyberpunk while fitting the Zepeto avatar style.

### Main Prompt
Your main prompt should include key elements such as:
- **Genre and Style**: Specify "cyberpunk" to ensure the outfit has a futuristic, tech-noir feel.
- **Platform**: Mention "for Zepeto" to tailor the design to the platform's avatar proportions and style.
- **Desired Features**: Include details like "neon lights," "metallic textures," and "futuristic designs" to achieve the desired aesthetic.
- **Level of Detail**: Request "intricate details and accessories" for a more complex and engaging design.

Example:
`Generate a high-fashion cyberpunk cyberwear outfit for a Zepeto avatar, incorporating neon lights, metallic textures, and futuristic designs, with a focus on intricate details and accessories.`

### Negative Prompt
To refine your results and avoid unwanted elements, use a negative prompt. This should exclude:
- **Realistic Human Skin**: To keep the focus on the cyberwear and maintain a stylized look.
- **Explicit Content**: Ensuring the design is appropriate for all audiences.
- **Copyrighted Material**: Avoiding any legal issues by excluding copyrighted logos or trademarks.

Example:
`Avoid generating outfits with realistic human skin, excessive blood or gore, or explicit content, and exclude any copyrighted logos or trademarks.`

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the best results, adjust your AI generation settings as follows:
- **Sampler**: `Euler a` for a balance between quality and diversity.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance)**: Set to `7.5` for a strong guidance towards your prompt without overfitting.
- **Steps**: `50` steps should provide a good balance between detail and processing time.

## Monetization Tips
Once you've generated your cyberpunk cyberwear designs, consider the following strategies to monetize them:

- **Custom Commissions**: Offer bespoke cyberwear designs for clients seeking unique outfits.
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with popular Zepeto influencers to showcase your creations, expanding your audience and potential client base.
