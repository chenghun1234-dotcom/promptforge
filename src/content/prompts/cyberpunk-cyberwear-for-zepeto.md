## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a popular trend in the world of digital fashion, and Zepeto is one of the leading platforms for creators to showcase and sell their unique designs. With the help of AI generation, pro creators can produce stunning, futuristic outfits that cater to the cyberpunk aesthetic.

## Understanding the Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto, it's essential to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI model. The main prompt should focus on the desired style, theme, and design elements, while the negative prompt should exclude unwanted features.

### Main Prompt
The main prompt should include key elements such as:
* Futuristic and cyberpunk-inspired design
* Metallic accents and neon lights
* Avant-garde design elements, such as bold shapes and unconventional materials

### Negative Prompt
The negative prompt should exclude features like:
* Realistic fabrics and textures
* Mundane colors and patterns
* Traditional clothing designs and silhouettes

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the desired results, it's crucial to adjust the technical settings for the AI generation process.

* **Sampler:** Euler a is a suitable sampler for generating cyberpunk cyberwear, as it produces high-quality images with intricate details.
* **CFG:** A CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance) value of 7.5 provides a good balance between creativity and control, allowing the AI model to generate unique designs while maintaining consistency with the prompt.
* **Steps:** 50 steps are sufficient for generating high-quality images, but you can adjust this value depending on your specific needs and computational resources.
