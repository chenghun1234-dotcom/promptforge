The cyberpunk genre has gained immense popularity in recent years, and its influence can be seen in various forms of media, including fashion. Creating cyberpunk-themed digital assets for platforms like Zepeto can be a lucrative business, especially when done with precision and attention to detail. This guide will walk you through the process of generating high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear using AI.

### Pro Prompt
The key to generating exceptional digital assets lies in crafting the perfect prompt. For cyberpunk cyberwear, consider the following:
- **Main Prompt**: Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek lines, with a focus on high-fashion and avant-garde design elements.
- **Negative Prompt**: Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of textures, or uninspired color palettes, and do not include any copyrighted or trademarked logos or symbols.

### Technical Settings
To achieve the desired outcome, adjust your AI generation settings as follows:
- **Sampler**: DDIM (Denoising Diffusion Implicit Model) for more controlled and detailed outputs.
- **CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance)**: 7.5 to balance between creativity and adherence to the prompt.
- **Steps**: 50 for a thorough and detailed generation process.

- Create bundles or collections that include multiple outfits or accessories at a discounted price to attract high-end buyers and fashion enthusiasts.
