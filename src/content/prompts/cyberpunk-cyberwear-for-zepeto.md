## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate exceptional cyberpunk cyberwear, it's essential to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI model. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, including metallic accents, neon lights, and sleek designs. The negative prompt should exclude unwanted features, such as simplistic designs or cartoonish elements.

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the desired results, it's crucial to configure the technical settings correctly. The recommended settings are:
* Sampler: Euler a
* CFG: 7.5
* Steps: 50

These settings will allow the AI model to generate detailed, high-quality images with a good balance between creativity and control.
