## Understanding the Prompt
The key to generating high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear lies in crafting a precise and detailed prompt. The main prompt should guide the AI towards creating an outfit that embodies the essence of cyberpunk: futuristic, neon-lit, metallic, and sleek. The negative prompt is equally important as it helps avoid common pitfalls such as simplistic designs or copyrighted materials.

## Technical Settings
- **Sampler:** DDIM (Denoising Diffusion Implicit Model) is recommended for its ability to produce highly detailed and realistic images with less distortion.
- **CFG:** A CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance) scale of 7.5 offers a good balance between creativity and adherence to the prompt, allowing for unique and varied designs without straying too far from the desired cyberpunk aesthetic.
- **Steps:** 50 steps provide ample iterations for the AI to refine the design, ensuring that the final product is of high quality and closely matches the prompt specifications.
