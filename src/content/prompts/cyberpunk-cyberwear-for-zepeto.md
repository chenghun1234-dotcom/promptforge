## Pro Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you'll need to craft a well-defined prompt that includes the following elements:
* A clear description of the desired outfit, including the style, colors, and textures
* Inspiration from existing cyberpunk franchises, such as Blade Runner or Ghost in the Shell
* A negative prompt to avoid generating outfits with undesirable characteristics

Example:
`Generate a high-fashion cyberpunk cyberwear outfit for Zepeto, with neon lights, metallic accents, and futuristic details, inspired by the likes of Blade Runner and Ghost in the Shell. Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of texture, or uninspired color palettes, and do not include any real-world logos or trademarks.`
