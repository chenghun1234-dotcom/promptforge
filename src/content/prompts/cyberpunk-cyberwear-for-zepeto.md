Cyberpunk cyberwear is a popular trend in the Zepeto community, with users seeking out futuristic and high-tech outfits to express their virtual personas. As a professional creator, you can capitalize on this demand by generating stunning cyberpunk-inspired designs using AI.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you need to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI generator. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, including:
* Futuristic and avant-garde designs
* Neon-lit accents and metallic textures
* Sleek and high-tech appearances

The negative prompt should exclude unwanted elements, such as:
* Bright colors and cartoonish designs
* Overly simplistic textures
* Logos or branding that may infringe on existing intellectual properties
