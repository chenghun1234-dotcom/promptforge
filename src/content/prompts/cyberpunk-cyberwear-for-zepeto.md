## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create a successful cyberpunk cyberwear asset, you need to craft a well-structured prompt that includes the following elements:
* A clear description of the desired outfit, including the type of clothing, accessories, and colors.
* A specific reference to the cyberpunk genre and its characteristic themes, such as neon lights, metallic surfaces, and futuristic designs.
* A mention of the desired level of detail and realism, to ensure that the generated asset meets your quality standards.
