To create stunning cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto, follow this professional AI prompt guide:

### Introduction
The demand for unique digital assets, especially in the realm of cyberpunk fashion, is on the rise. Zepeto, a popular metaverse platform, offers a vast market for creators to sell their custom-made digital clothing items. By leveraging AI generation tools like Stable Diffusion, you can produce high-quality, cyberpunk-inspired outfits that appeal to a wide range of customers.

### Technical Settings
For optimal results, use the following technical settings:
- **Sampler:** Euler a
- **CFG:** 7.5
- **Steps:** 50

These settings will help you achieve a balance between detail and coherence in your generated images.

### Pro Prompt
The key to successful AI generation lies in crafting a well-defined prompt. For cyberpunk cyberwear, use the following:
- **Main Prompt:** Generate a highly detailed cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and futuristic patterns, with a focus on sleek and high-tech design elements.
- **Negative Prompt:** Avoid generating historical, fantasy, or minimalist clothing styles, and do not include any real-world logos or copyrighted materials.

This prompt combination will guide the AI to produce outfits that are distinctly cyberpunk and suitable for the Zepeto platform.

### Monetization Tip
To turn your generated cyberpunk cyberwear into a profitable venture:
- Sell your assets on online marketplaces like the Zepeto store, TurboSquid, or Daz3D.
- Offer custom commission services to clients looking for unique, bespoke digital fashion items. This can include tailored designs based on client preferences, exclusive materials, or specific themes.
- Engage with the Zepeto community and other digital creators to showcase your work, gather feedback, and stay updated on the latest trends and demands.

By following this guide, you can tap into the thriving market of digital cyberpunk fashion and establish yourself as a professional creator of unique and captivating cyberwear assets for Zepeto.
