## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To get started, you'll need to craft a well-structured prompt that captures the essence of cyberpunk fashion. Our recommended prompt is:
"Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde design elements, with a focus on high-fashion and cutting-edge style."
Be sure to pair this prompt with a negative prompt that discourages the AI from generating outfits with overly simplistic designs or lackluster colors:
"Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lackluster colors, or inadequate attention to detail, and do not include any real-world brand logos or copyrighted materials."
