To create stunning cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto, follow these professional AI prompt guidelines:

### Introduction
The cyberpunk genre has gained immense popularity, and creating digital assets that fit this aesthetic can be lucrative. Zepeto, a popular metaverse platform, allows users to customize their avatars with unique outfits. By generating cyberpunk-inspired cyberwear, you can tap into this market and sell your digital assets to enthusiastic buyers.

### Pro Prompt
The key to generating high-quality cyberwear assets lies in crafting a well-defined prompt. Your main prompt should include:
- A clear description of the desired outfit, focusing on cyberpunk elements such as neon accents, metallic textures, and circuitry patterns.
- An emphasis on avant-garde fashion and cutting-edge technology to ensure the outfit stands out.

Your negative prompt should exclude:
- Simplistic designs that lack detail and flair.
- Outfits without neon accents, metallic textures, or circuitry patterns, as these are essential to the cyberpunk aesthetic.
- Any real-world logos or copyrighted materials to avoid legal issues.

### Technical Settings
To achieve the best results, use the following technical settings:
- **Sampler:** Euler a for its ability to generate detailed, high-quality images.
- **CFG:** 7.5 to balance quality and diversity in the generated assets.
- **Steps:** 50 to allow for sufficient iterations and refinement of the generated image.

### Monetization Tip
To successfully sell your cyberpunk cyberwear assets, consider the following:
- **Platform:** Utilize Zepeto's official marketplace, as well as other popular platforms like TurboSquid or Daz3D, to reach a broad audience.
- **Customization:** Offer customization options for your assets, such as different color schemes or accessory packs, to attract more buyers and increase revenue potential.
- **Marketing:** Engage with the Zepeto community and cyberpunk enthusiasts on social media to showcase your assets and build a customer base.

By following this guide, you can create and sell unique cyberpunk cyberwear assets for Zepeto, capitalizing on the popularity of the cyberpunk genre and the growing demand for digital fashion items in metaverse platforms.
