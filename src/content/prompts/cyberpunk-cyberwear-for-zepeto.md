## Understanding the Prompt
The prompt is the foundation of your AI-generated cyberwear. It needs to be detailed enough to guide the AI towards your vision but open-ended enough to allow for creative freedom. The main prompt focuses on generating a futuristic, cyberpunk-inspired outfit with specific characteristics such as neon accents, metallic textures, and sleek lines. The negative prompt ensures that the AI avoids certain elements that might detract from the cyberpunk aesthetic, such as overly simplistic designs or bright colors.

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
- **Sampler:** Euler a is chosen for its ability to produce high-quality images with detailed textures and patterns, which is crucial for capturing the complexity of cyberpunk designs.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A CFG of 7.5 is used to balance between the guidance of the prompt and the creativity of the model. This allows for a good mix of adherence to the prompt and exploration of the cyberpunk theme.
- **Steps:** 50 steps are sufficient to generate images with a high level of detail and coherence, ensuring that the cyberwear designs are well-defined and visually appealing.
