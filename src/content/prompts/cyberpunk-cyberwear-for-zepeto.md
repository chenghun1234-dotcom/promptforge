### Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto

Creating unique and captivating cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto characters can be a lucrative venture for professional creators. ### Crafting the Perfect Prompt

To generate exceptional cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be detailed and specific. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, such as neon accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde accessories. It's also crucial to define what you want to avoid in your generation through a well-crafted negative prompt.

#### Example Prompt:
Generate a highly detailed, futuristic cyberpunk outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde accessories, with a focus on sleek, high-tech aesthetics and bold, vibrant colors.

#### Example Negative Prompt:
Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, mundane colors, or lack of futuristic elements, and do not include any real-world brand logos or copyrighted materials.

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results
