## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
The demand for unique and stylish digital assets in virtual worlds like Zepeto is on the rise. Cyberpunk-themed cyberwear, with its blend of futuristic, high-tech, and often neon-lit aesthetics, presents a compelling opportunity for creators to cater to this demand. By leveraging AI generation tools, professionals can create intricate, detailed, and highly customizable cyberpunk outfits that appeal to a wide range of Zepeto users.

## Crafting Your Pro Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, it's essential to craft a prompt that is both specific and inspiring for the AI model. The main prompt should focus on the core elements of cyberpunk fashion, including neon accents, metallic textures, and futuristic designs. The negative prompt is equally important, as it helps the AI avoid common pitfalls such as simplicity, lack of thematic adherence, and similarity to existing assets.

### Main Prompt
- **Theme and Aesthetic:** Cyberpunk with an emphasis on neon and metallic elements.
- **Design Complexity:** Highly detailed with versatile and customizable parts for different avatars.
- **Inspiration:** Draw from iconic cyberpunk franchises, modern fashion trends, and futuristic concepts.

### Negative Prompt
- **Simplicity:** Avoid outfits that lack detail or complexity.
- **Theme Adherence:** Ensure the design strictly adheres to the cyberpunk theme, avoiding elements that are too futuristic or sci-fi without a cyberpunk context.
- **Originality:** Steer clear of designs that are overly similar to existing Zepeto assets or well-known cyberpunk characters.

## Technical Settings for Generation
To achieve the best results, the following technical settings are recommended:
- **Sampler:** Euler a, for its ability to produce detailed and coherent images.
- **CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance):** 7.5, to balance fidelity and creativity.
- **Steps:** 50, allowing for a thorough exploration of the design space without overfitting.

- **Influencer Collaborations:** Partner with popular Zepeto influencers to showcase your cyberwear, reaching a broader audience and leveraging the influencers' credibility and fanbase.
