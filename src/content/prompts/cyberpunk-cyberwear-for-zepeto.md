### Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create compelling cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt should evoke a sense of futuristic sophistication while avoiding elements that detract from the cyberpunk theme. 

- **Main Prompt:** `Create a futuristic, neon-lit cyberpunk outfit for a Zepeto character, complete with metallic accents, glowing circuit patterns, and sleek, high-tech accessories.`
- **Negative Prompt:** `Avoid creating outfits with overly simplistic designs, bright pastel colors, or elements that resemble historical or traditional clothing.`

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results
Achieving the desired level of detail and aesthetic in your cyberwear designs requires careful adjustment of your AI model's technical settings.

- **Sampler:** Euler a offers a good balance between detail and speed for creating intricate, high-tech designs.
- **CFG:** A setting of 7.5 provides a strong guidance towards the prompt without overfitting, allowing for creative freedom within the cyberpunk theme.
- **Steps:** 50 steps are sufficient for most designs, offering a detailed outcome without significantly increasing rendering time.
