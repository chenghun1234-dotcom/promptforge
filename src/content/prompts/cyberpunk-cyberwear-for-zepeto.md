## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk, as a genre, embodies the fusion of futuristic technology with a gritty, high-tech aesthetic, making it a perfect fit for digital platforms like Zepeto. Creating cyberpunk cyberwear involves blending neon accents, metallic textures, and intricate circuitry details into outfits that are both visually striking and reflective of an avant-garde, futuristic vibe.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate compelling cyberpunk cyberwear, it's essential to craft a prompt that is both descriptive and focused. The main prompt should outline the core elements of the desired design, including the genre, specific features like neon accents or metallic textures, and the overall aesthetic aim, such as sleek and avant-garde. A negative prompt helps refine the generation by excluding undesired elements, ensuring the outcome is as envisioned.

## Technical Settings for Generation
- **Sampler:** Euler a is recommended for its ability to produce detailed, high-quality images with a good balance between speed and quality.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A CFG of 7.5 offers a strong guidance towards the prompt without overfitting, allowing for creative variations within the bounds of the described cyberpunk theme.
- **Steps:** 50 steps provide ample iterations for the model to refine the image, ensuring that the final product is detailed and aligns closely with the prompt's specifications.
