## Introduction
As a professional creator of digital assets, staying ahead of the curve in terms of style and trend is crucial. Cyberpunk, with its blend of futuristic and high-tech elements, offers a rich canvas for creativity. This guide is designed to help you create stunning cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto using AI, and provide insights into how you can monetize these assets.

## Understanding the Prompt
The key to generating high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear lies in crafting a precise prompt that guides the AI model towards your desired outcome. The prompt should include key elements such as:
- **Futuristic and Cyberpunk Elements**: Neon lights, metallic finishes, and sleek designs are staples of the cyberpunk aesthetic.
- **Intricate Details**: Encourage the AI to focus on creating outfits with detailed accessories and embellishments.
- **Bold and Vibrant Colors**: Cyberpunk fashion often features a palette of bold, neon colors against darker backgrounds.

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the best results, consider the following technical settings:
- **Sampler**: Euler a offers a good balance between detail and speed for generating complex designs like cyberpunk outfits.
- **CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance)**: A CFG of 7.5 provides a strong guidance towards the prompt without overfitting, allowing for creative freedom within the cyberpunk theme.
- **Steps**: 50 steps are sufficient for most designs, but you may need to adjust based on the complexity of your prompt and the desired level of detail.
