## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk, as a genre, has always fascinated with its blend of high-tech and low-life elements, offering a rich canvas for fashion designers to explore. When it comes to creating cyberwear for platforms like Zepeto, the challenge lies in balancing futuristic elements with wearable, stylish designs that appeal to a wide audience. This guide is designed to help professional creators leverage AI in crafting unique, captivating cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate stunning cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt is crucial. It needs to convey the essence of cyberpunk fashion while guiding the AI toward producing highly detailed, avant-garde designs.

- **Main Prompt**: `Generate a highly detailed cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and futuristic patterns, with a focus on avant-garde fashion and cutting-edge technology integration.`
- **Negative Prompt**: `Avoid generating outfits that are too simplistic, lack neon or metallic elements, or resemble historical or contemporary clothing styles not associated with cyberpunk.`

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
To achieve the best results, it's essential to fine-tune your AI's technical settings.

- **Sampler**: Euler a offers a good balance between detail and diversity in generated images.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance)**: A CFG of 7.5 provides a strong guidance towards the prompt without overfitting.
- **Steps**: 50 steps allow for a thorough exploration of the design space without compromising on detail.

- **Targeted Marketing**: Focus your marketing efforts on communities and forums where science fiction and cyberpunk fans congregate, highlighting the futuristic and avant-garde aspects of your designs.
