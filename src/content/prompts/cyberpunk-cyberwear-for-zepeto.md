### Pro Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, it's essential to craft a well-defined prompt that guides the AI model towards your vision. The main prompt should include key elements such as futuristic themes, neon accents, and metallic textures, while the negative prompt helps to refine the output by excluding unwanted features.

- **Main Prompt:** Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek designs, with a focus on avant-garde fashion and cutting-edge technology.
- **Negative Prompt:** Avoid generating outfits with excessive logos, cartoonish elements, or overly simplistic designs, and exclude any references to existing brands or franchises.
