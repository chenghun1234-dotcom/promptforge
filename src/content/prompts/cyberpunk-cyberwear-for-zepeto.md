### Pro Prompt
The key to creating captivating cyberpunk cyberwear lies in the prompt. Our expert-crafted prompt is designed to elicit bold, vibrant, and high-tech designs from the AI model.

**Main Prompt:** Design a futuristic cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto avatar, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek, high-tech accessories, with a focus on bold, vibrant colors and dynamic poses.
