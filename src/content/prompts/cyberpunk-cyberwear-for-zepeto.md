## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
The demand for unique and captivating digital assets, especially in the realm of cyberpunk-themed content, has seen a significant surge. For professional creators looking to capitalize on this trend, generating cyberpunk cyberwear for platforms like Zepeto can be a lucrative venture. This guide will walk you through the process of creating stunning cyberpunk cyberwear using AI, along with tips on how to monetize your creations.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be precise and detailed. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, including neon lights, metallic textures, and futuristic designs. The negative prompt is equally important, as it helps the AI avoid common pitfalls such as simplistic designs or non-cyberpunk elements.

### Pro Prompt Example
- **Main Prompt:** Generate a highly detailed cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit jackets, metallic pants, and futuristic accessories, with a focus on vibrant colors and sleek designs.
- **Negative Prompt:** Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, minimal details, or lackluster color schemes, and exclude any elements that may be considered outdated or non-cyberpunk.

## Technical Settings for Optimal Results
The technical settings of your AI generation tool can significantly impact the quality and aesthetic of your cyberwear designs. Here are some recommended settings for achieving the best results:

- **Sampler:** Euler a
- **CFG:** 7.5
- **Steps:** 50

These settings can help you achieve detailed, vibrant, and futuristic designs that fit perfectly within the cyberpunk genre.
