### Crafting the Perfect AI Prompt
To create captivating cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be detailed and specific. The main prompt should evoke a sense of futurism, technology, and avant-garde style, while the negative prompt ensures that the output stays within the desired theme and avoids undesirable elements.

- **Main Prompt**: `Create a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde accessories, with a focus on sleek, high-tech design elements and bold, vibrant colors.`
- **Negative Prompt**: `Avoid creating outfits with overly simplistic designs, muted colors, or lack of futuristic elements, and do not include any realistic or historical references.`

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results
Achieving the perfect cyberpunk look requires the right balance of technical settings. These settings can significantly impact the quality and aesthetic of your final design.

- **Sampler**: Euler a is recommended for its ability to generate detailed and coherent images, which is essential for capturing the intricate designs often found in cyberpunk fashion.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance)**: A CFG of 7.5 offers a good balance between guidance and creativity, allowing the model to adhere closely to your prompt while still introducing unique elements.
- **Steps**: 50 steps provide enough iterations for the model to refine the design and incorporate the subtle details that define cyberpunk style.
