## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
As a professional creator of digital assets, you're likely familiar with the growing demand for unique and stylish outfits in virtual worlds like Zepeto. Cyberpunk-inspired cyberwear is a particularly popular trend, offering a fusion of futuristic and high-fashion elements that appeal to a wide range of users. ## Crafting the Perfect AI Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you'll need to craft a well-structured AI prompt that includes the following elements:
* A clear description of the desired outfit, including the theme, style, and key features.
* A focus on high-fashion and avant-garde design elements to ensure the outfit stands out.
* A negative prompt to avoid generating outfits with undesirable characteristics.

Example:
```markdown
Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and futuristic patterns, with a focus on high-fashion and avant-garde design elements.
```
Negative prompt:
```markdown
Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of texture, or uninspired color palettes, and do not include any real-world logos or branding.
```

## Technical Settings for AI Generation
