The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, such as neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek designs. **Main Prompt:** Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek designs, with a focus on avant-garde fashion and innovative materials.
