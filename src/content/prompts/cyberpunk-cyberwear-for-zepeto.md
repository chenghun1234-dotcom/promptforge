### Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto

The cyberpunk genre has long been a staple of science fiction, and its influence on fashion is undeniable. For creators looking to tap into this aesthetic for Zepeto characters, understanding how to generate high-quality, cyberpunk-inspired digital assets is crucial. This guide is designed to help professional creators navigate the process of generating stunning cyberpunk cyberwear using AI tools.

### Crafting the Perfect Prompt

To generate exceptional cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be detailed and specific. The main prompt should include elements such as:

- **Futuristic and avant-garde design**: Emphasize the use of neon accents, metallic textures, and unique accessories that reflect the cyberpunk theme.
- **Vibrant colors and dynamic lighting**: Specify the importance of vibrant, neon colors and how lighting should enhance the overall futuristic feel of the outfit.
- **Zepeto character compatibility**: Ensure that the generated outfit is compatible with Zepeto characters, considering their proportions and customization options.

The negative prompt is just as important, as it helps avoid unwanted elements in your generation. Key points to include are:

- **Avoid realistic or historical elements**: The focus should be on futuristic, sci-fi elements that align with the cyberpunk genre.
- **No copyrighted or trademarked symbols**: Ensure that your design does not infringe on any existing copyrights or trademarks to avoid legal issues.

### Technical Settings for Optimal Generation

For the best results, consider the following technical settings:

- **Sampler**: Euler a is often preferred for its ability to generate detailed and coherent images.
- **CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance)**: A CFG of 7.5 can provide a good balance between guidance and creativity, allowing for unique and detailed cyberpunk designs.
- **Steps**: 50 steps can offer a thorough exploration of the design space, leading to more refined and intricate cyberpunk cyberwear.
