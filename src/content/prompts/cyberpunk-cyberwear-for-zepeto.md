## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk, as a genre, offers a rich tapestry of neon-drenched landscapes, high-tech gadgetry, and a blend of dystopian grit with futuristic glamour. When it comes to cyberwear for avatars in platforms like Zepeto, the challenge lies in capturing the essence of this aesthetic while ensuring the designs are not only visually striking but also wearable and appealing to a wide audience. This guide is designed to help professional creators navigate the process of generating compelling cyberpunk cyberwear using AI tools, with a focus on selling these digital assets.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
The key to successful AI-generated content is the prompt. For cyberpunk cyberwear, the goal is to evoke a sense of futuristic rebellion, incorporating elements like neon lights, metallic sheens, and avant-garde silhouettes. The **main prompt** should guide the AI towards this vision:
"Generate a futuristic cyberpunk outfit for Zepeto, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde silhouettes, with a focus on high-fashion appeal and intricate details."

To refine the output and avoid undesirable elements, a **negative prompt** is essential:
"Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of texture, or elements that do not fit within the cyberpunk aesthetic, such as bright pastel colors or overly casual wear."

## Technical Settings for Optimal Results
- **Sampler:** Euler a offers a good balance between detail and coherence, making it suitable for generating complex, high-fashion cyberwear.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A CFG of 7.5 provides a strong guidance towards the desired cyberpunk features without overfitting or losing creativity.
- **Steps:** 50 steps allow for a detailed exploration of the design space, ensuring intricate details and a polished final product.

- **Bundles and Collections:** Package coordinating outfits or themed collections to appeal to collectors and enthusiasts, potentially increasing average sale values.
