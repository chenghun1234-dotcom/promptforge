## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a highly sought-after aesthetic in the world of Zepeto, with its blend of futuristic and high-tech elements. As a professional creator, you can capitalize on this trend by generating unique and stunning cyberpunk cyberwear outfits using AI.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you need a well-crafted prompt that captures the essence of the aesthetic. The main prompt should include key elements such as:
* High-fashion cyberpunk cyberwear outfit
* Neon accents
* Metallic textures
* Futuristic aesthetic
* Inspiration from iconic cyberpunk franchises like Ghost in the Shell and Blade Runner

The negative prompt should exclude elements that do not fit the cyberpunk theme, such as:
* Cluttered or overly complex designs
* Historical or traditional clothing items
* Non-cyberpunk themed elements

To achieve the best results, use the following technical settings:

These settings will allow you to generate high-quality, detailed outfits with a good balance between creativity and control.
