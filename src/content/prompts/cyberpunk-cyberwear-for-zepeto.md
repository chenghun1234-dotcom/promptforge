## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, your prompt needs to be detailed and specific. The main prompt should focus on the key elements of cyberpunk fashion, such as metallic accents, neon lights, and sleek designs. The negative prompt is equally important, as it helps avoid common pitfalls like simplistic designs or unauthorized branding.

### Example Prompt
- **Main Prompt:** Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto avatar, including metallic accents, neon lights, and sleek designs, with a focus on high-fashion and avant-garde styles.
- **Negative Prompt:** Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, lack of texture, or insufficient attention to detail, and do not include any logos or branding from existing companies.
