### Crafting the Perfect Prompt

To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, your AI prompt needs to be detailed and specific. The **main prompt** should guide the AI towards creating an outfit that embodies the essence of cyberpunk fashion, including elements such as neon accents, metallic textures, and intricate circuitry patterns. Meanwhile, the **negative prompt** is crucial for avoiding common pitfalls like simplistic designs or the inclusion of elements that detract from the desired aesthetic.

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results

Achieving the desired level of detail and realism in your cyberpunk cyberwear designs requires the right technical settings:

- **Sampler:** Utilize the `DDIM` sampler for its ability to produce high-quality images with a good balance between detail and coherence.
- **CFG:** A `CFG` scale of `7.5` strikes a balance between adhering to the prompt and allowing for creative freedom, which is ideal for generating unique and interesting designs.
- **Steps:** Setting the number of steps to `50` provides the AI with sufficient iterations to refine the design, ensuring that the final product is detailed and polished.
