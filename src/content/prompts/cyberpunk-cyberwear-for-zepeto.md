### Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto

The world of Zepeto, a popular metaverse platform, is ripe with opportunities for creators to design and sell unique digital assets, including cyberpunk-inspired cyberwear. This guide is tailored for professional creators looking to tap into the vibrant market of Zepeto's fashion-conscious community by leveraging AI-powered design tools.

### Crafting the Perfect Prompt

To create stunning cyberpunk cyberwear, it's essential to craft a prompt that guides the AI model towards generating designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly detailed and unique. The main prompt should evoke the essence of cyberpunk, focusing on futuristic elements, neon lights, and avant-garde styles.

**Main Prompt:** "Design a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde accessories, with a focus on high-fashion appeal and intricate details."

**Negative Prompt:** To ensure the AI model avoids common pitfalls, a well-structured negative prompt is crucial. This should discourage the generation of outfits that are too simplistic or lackluster. "Avoid designing outfits with overly simplistic patterns, lack of texture, or uninspired color schemes, and refrain from incorporating real-world logos or copyrighted materials."

### Technical Settings for Optimal Results

Achieving the desired outcome involves more than just a well-crafted prompt. The technical settings of the AI model play a significant role in the quality and uniqueness of the generated designs.

- **Sampler:** The choice of sampler can significantly affect the diversity and quality of the generated images. For cyberpunk cyberwear, the "Euler a" sampler is recommended for its ability to produce detailed and coherent results.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A CFG value of 7.5 is suggested as it provides a good balance between adhering to the prompt and allowing for creative freedom, which is essential for generating unique and interesting cyberpunk designs.
- **Steps:** The number of steps can influence the level of detail and refinement in the generated images. For intricate cyberpunk cyberwear, 50 steps are recommended to ensure that the designs are not only detailed but also well-refined.
