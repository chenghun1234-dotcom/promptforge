## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
The cyberpunk genre has seen a resurgence in popularity, and Zepeto avatars are the perfect canvas for expressing this futuristic, high-tech aesthetic. As a professional creator selling digital assets, you can capitalize on this trend by designing stunning cyberpunk-inspired outfits for Zepeto avatars.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To create high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear designs, you need a well-crafted prompt that guides the AI model toward your desired outcome. The main prompt should evoke the essence of cyberpunk, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek accessories. However, it's equally important to define what you don't want through a negative prompt, ensuring the AI avoids bright colors, cartoonish elements, and real-world brand logos.

### Main Prompt
The main prompt is designed to inspire the AI to generate futuristic, cyberpunk-themed outfits. Consider the elements that define cyberpunk fashion, such as:
- Neon lights and accents
- Metallic and reflective surfaces
- High-tech gadgets and accessories
- Futuristic materials and textures

### Negative Prompt
The negative prompt is crucial for refining the AI's output, ensuring it stays within the desired aesthetic boundaries. Key elements to avoid include:
- Bright, overly vibrant colors
- Cartoonish or simplistic design elements
- Real-world brand logos or copyrighted materials

## Technical Settings for Optimal Results
Achieving the perfect cyberpunk cyberwear design requires not only a well-crafted prompt but also the right technical settings.

- **Sampler:** DDIM (Denoising Diffusion Implicit Model) is recommended for its ability to produce high-quality, detailed images with less noise.
- **CFG (Classifier Free Guidance):** A CFG of 7.5 offers a good balance between guidance and creativity, allowing the model to generate unique designs while adhering to the prompt.
- **Steps:** 50 steps provide ample iterations for the model to refine and detail the design, resulting in a more polished and sophisticated output.
