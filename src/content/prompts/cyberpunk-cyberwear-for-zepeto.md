## Introduction to Cyberpunk Cyberwear for Zepeto
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a popular trend in the Zepeto community, with many users seeking unique and futuristic outfits for their characters. As a professional creator, you can capitalize on this demand by generating stunning cyberpunk-inspired cyberwear using AI.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you'll need to craft a well-structured prompt that includes key elements such as:
* Futuristic themes
* Neon-lit accents
* Metallic textures
* Sleek, high-tech accessories

Example:
```markdown
Generate a futuristic cyberpunk-inspired outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and sleek, high-tech accessories.
```

## Refining Your Prompt with Negative Keywords
To avoid generating outfits that don't fit your cyberpunk theme, include negative keywords that discourage the AI from producing undesirable results. For example:
* Overly simplistic designs
* Bright pastel colors
* Lack of futuristic elements

Example:
```markdown
Avoid generating outfits with overly simplistic designs, bright pastel colors, or lack of futuristic elements.
```
