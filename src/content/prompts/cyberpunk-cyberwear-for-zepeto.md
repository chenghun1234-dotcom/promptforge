## Introduction
Cyberpunk cyberwear is a highly sought-after aesthetic in the world of Zepeto, and as a professional creator, you can capitalize on this trend by generating stunning, high-fashion outfits using AI. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of creating cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto, from crafting the perfect prompt to monetizing your digital assets.

## Crafting the Perfect Prompt
To generate high-quality cyberpunk cyberwear, you'll need to craft a prompt that effectively communicates your vision to the AI model. The main prompt should include key elements such as:
* Futuristic and cyberpunk-inspired themes
* Neon-lit accents and metallic textures
* Sleek lines and high-fashion design elements
* Avant-garde and experimental approaches to fashion

The negative prompt should exclude elements that might detract from the overall aesthetic, such as:
* Overly simplistic or generic designs
* Logos, trademarks, or copyrighted materials

## Technical Settings
To achieve the best results, use the following technical settings:
* **Sampler:** Euler a
* **CFG:** 7.5
* **Steps:** 50

These settings will allow the AI model to generate highly detailed and realistic outfits with a focus on texture and lighting.

## Monetization
Once you've generated your cyberpunk cyberwear, you can monetize it by selling it as exclusive digital assets on platforms like the Zepeto marketplace. You can also offer custom commission services to clients looking for unique, high-end cyberpunk-inspired outfits for their characters. Consider offering bundles or discounts for bulk purchases, and make sure to showcase your creations in a visually appealing way to attract potential buyers.
