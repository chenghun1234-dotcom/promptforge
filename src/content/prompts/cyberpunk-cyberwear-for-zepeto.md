To create stunning cyberpunk cyberwear for Zepeto using AI, follow this professional guide:

### Introduction
The cyberpunk genre has always been a staple of futuristic fashion, and Zepeto's digital platform is the perfect place to showcase your unique cyberwear designs. With the help of AI, you can generate high-quality, cutting-edge outfits that will make your characters stand out.

### Pro Prompt
The key to creating amazing cyberpunk cyberwear is to craft a well-structured prompt that guides the AI model towards your desired outcome. Use the following prompt:
**Main Prompt:** Design a futuristic cyberpunk-themed outfit for a Zepeto character, including neon-lit accents, metallic textures, and avant-garde accessories, with a focus on high-fashion and cutting-edge technology integration.
**Negative Prompt:** Avoid using bright colors, cartoonish elements, or overly simplistic designs, and do not include any real-world brand logos or copyrighted materials.

### Technical Settings
To achieve the best results, use the following technical settings:
* **Sampler:** DDIM (Denoising Diffusion Implicit Model)
* **CFG:** 7.5 (This will control the level of detail and realism in your generated image)
* **Steps:** 50 (This will determine the number of iterations the AI model will perform to refine your design)

Sell your unique cyberpunk cyberwear designs as exclusive digital assets on platforms like the Zepeto marketplace, or offer custom commissions to clients looking for one-of-a-kind outfits.
