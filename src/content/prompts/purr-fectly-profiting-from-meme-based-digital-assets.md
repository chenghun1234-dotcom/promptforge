## 💰 Monetization Strategy
To sell images generated with this prompt on platforms like Adobe Stock or Zepeto, consider creating a series of related assets that cater to different themes or occasions, such as holidays or trending topics. For example, you could generate variations of the cute cat meme with different military or historical backdrops, and then bundle them into a single package or collection, making it easy for potential buyers to purchase and utilize the entire set. This approach can help increase the perceived value of your assets and attract more customers, ultimately leading to higher sales and revenue.
