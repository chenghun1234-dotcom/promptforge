To capitalize on the popularity of meme-style avatar illustrations, consider creating a series of unique characters and selling them as exclusive digital assets on platforms like Adobe Stock or the Unity Asset Store. One actionable tip is to bundle your illustrations with complementary assets, such as matching backgrounds or accessories, to increase their appeal and value to potential buyers, particularly game developers and digital marketers looking to enhance their brand's online presence with distinctive and engaging visuals.
